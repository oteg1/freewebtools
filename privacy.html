Privacy Policy

Overview

FreeToolBox ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Data You Enter Into Tools

Most of our tools process your data entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Text you enter into tools like the word counter, JSON formatter, or hash generator is not sent to our servers
  • Passwords generated by our password generator are created locally and never transmitted
  • Your data remains on your device and is not stored by us

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we may collect certain information automatically when you visit our site:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • IP address (anonymized)

This information is used solely to improve our website and is not used to identify individual users.

Advertising

We use third-party advertising services (such as Google AdSense) to display ads on our website. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • DAA Opt-Out

Cookies

Our website may use cookies for:

  • Remembering your preferences (e.g., theme settings)
  • Analytics to understand how our site is used
  • Serving relevant advertisements

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website usage statistics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • CDN Services: For delivering JavaScript libraries (e.g., QR code library)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or alteration of your data. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all connections.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
