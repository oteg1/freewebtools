About FreeToolBox

Free, fast, and privacy-respecting online tools for everyone.

Our Mission

FreeToolBox provides a collection of useful online tools that are:

  • 100% Free - No premium tiers, no hidden costs
  • No Signup Required - Just use the tools, no account needed
  • Privacy-Focused - Most tools run entirely in your browser
  • Fast & Lightweight - No bloat, instant results
  • Ad-Supported - We use non-intrusive ads to keep the lights on

Our Tools

We offer tools across several categories:

  • Text Tools: Word counter, case converter, Lorem Ipsum generator, text diff
  • Developer Tools: JSON formatter, Base64 encoder, hash generator, URL encoder
  • Math Calculators: Percentage, tip, loan, age, and BMI calculators
  • Conversion Tools: Unit converter, color converter, temperature, time zones
  • Generators: Password, UUID, QR code, random numbers, countdown timer

Privacy First

We believe your data is yours. That's why:

  • Most tools process data entirely in your browser - nothing is sent to our servers
  • We don't track individual users or sell data
  • We use minimal, non-intrusive advertising
  • No cookies are required to use our tools
