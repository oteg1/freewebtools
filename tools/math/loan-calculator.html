How Loan Payments Work

Your monthly payment is calculated using this formula:

M = P * [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1]

Where:

  • M = Monthly payment
  • P = Principal (loan amount)
  • r = Monthly interest rate (annual rate / 12)
  • n = Total number of payments
