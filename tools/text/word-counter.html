How to Use

Simply paste or type your text in the box above. The tool automatically counts:

  • Words: Total number of words separated by spaces
  • Characters: Total characters including spaces
  • Characters (no spaces): Characters excluding spaces
  • Sentences: Based on period, question mark, and exclamation mark endings
  • Paragraphs: Sections separated by blank lines
  • Reading Time: Estimated time to read at 200 words per minute
